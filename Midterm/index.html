About Us

Our company provides modern and custom window coverings for both residential and commercial use.

Our in-home consultations last approximately 20-minutes to one hour. We will provide you with personalized recommendations for your home. All prices are contingent upon the materials, square footage, and style chosen.

We can provide commercial architectural specified estimates directly from plans and specifications. Our sales and project management team can handle any project size and timeline. With a variety of options and vendors we can find the product that fits your client with ease. We can work in both Union and non-Union jobs. Add us to your bidder's list and you will always receive a timely and accurate price.
